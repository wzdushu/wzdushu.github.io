About me

My online name is “Wanzishuo”. I am currently working in an artificial intelligence company and am responsible for operation and maintenance architecture. In my spare time, I like to read books and write blogs. I like to make friends through blogging.

For any questions about the blog, you can email me at wzdushu#gmail.com (replace # with @)

About this site

  • Site domain name – wnote.com, w means “Wanzi”, so wnote can be understood as Wanzi has a memory and a memory to remember.

  • Site comments – Using giscus, a comment system driven by GitHub Discussions, similar to gitment, gittalk, and utteranc.

  • Site certificate – Free SSL certificate from zerossl applied by ACME.

Daily life of Wanzi

  • Main operating system – Mac&&Linux, I like the feeling of typing commands and writing code on Mac anytime and anywhere.
  • Editor —— Vim&&VsCode&&Goland , vim is the standard text editor for Linux, VsCode is mainly used for front-end and Python script debugging, Goland is used for back-end project development
  • Programming language —— Familiarity from high to low shell, Python, Golang, Html/Css/Js .
  • Keyboard —— Cherry G80 Red Axis

Purpose of this website

  1. Technical summary. As time goes by, some technologies are quickly forgotten. Establishing an independent site to write a blog is not only convenient for me to find and drive myself to write a summary in the future, but also makes me take this matter more seriously.

  2. Sharing experience and ideas. I came to Beijing after graduation. Along the way, I also experienced many setbacks, but through the process of self-learning and growth, I quickly integrated into the wave of the Internet. Therefore, sharing some valuable things is also a kind of feedback to the industry and society.

  3. Making friends. In this era of rapid development, I think it is also an interesting thing to make friends through blogs.
